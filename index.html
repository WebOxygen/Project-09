What is Shapiro MD Custom Prescription Formula?

This prescription triple-action treatment serum is a game changer for women suffering from hair loss. This unique compound formula contains FDA-approved Minoxidil, superpowered by tretinoin. This creates a more efficient, streamlined approach to restoring your hair’s natural density.

How does it work?

This winning formula is extremely effective, thanks to its 2 powerhouse ingredients:

Minoxidil - Promotes healthy blood flow in your scalp, bringing more vitamins and oxygen to your hair follicles, driving them into “grow mode.”

Tretinoin - Removes stubborn scar tissue around your hair follicles, creating more optimal conditions for healthy hair growth.

How do you use it?

Apply approximately 1/4 teaspoon of this serum to your dry scalp once a day, in the morning or evening. Do not wet your hair for at least 4 hours after applying this medication.

Who should not take Shapiro MD Custom Prescription Formula?

This medication is designed specifically for women. If you are a man suffering from hair loss, we recommend using our special men’s formula. Click here to learn more.

When can I expect to see results?

While everyone’s experience is different, most users see a significant improvement in their hair within the first 6 months of using Shapiro MD Custom Prescription Solution.
